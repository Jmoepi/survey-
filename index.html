Q6
Did you know that paying UIF to SARS via your monthly EMP201 is a separate obligation from submitting monthly employee declarations to the Department of Labour (DOL)?
These are two distinct legal requirements under the Unemployment Insurance Act 63 of 2001.
Q7
How do you currently handle the monthly UI-19 declaration to the Department of Labour?
The UI-19 must be submitted to the DOL by the 7th of every month, listing all employees and their remuneration.
